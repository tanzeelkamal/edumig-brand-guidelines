01 — Brand Story

Who We Are

Edumig Limited was born from a single conviction: that every ambitious student in Bangladesh deserves world-class guidance — not guesswork, not luck. Founded in 2008 as the Innovative English Language Centre in Chattogram, it became the country's first ICEF-certified consultancy of its kind.

In 2021, it evolved into Edumig Limited — a comprehensive, end-to-end education consultancy with offices in Chattogram and Dhaka, partnered with 700+ universities across 25+ countries.

We handle everything: language training, university selection, application processing, visa assistance, and flight arrangements. From first conversation to departure gate — we are there.

"We are more than consultants — we are enablers of dreams."

Over 5,000 students have trusted Edumig with their futures. That trust is not a statistic. It is our mandate and our standard.

Excellence & Quality
The highest standard in every service, every interaction. Non-negotiable.
Integrity & Transparency
Honesty and clarity in every conversation. Trust is earned, not assumed.
Innovation & Adaptability
Cutting-edge tools and approaches. We stay ahead so students don't fall behind.
Client-Centric Approach
Every student is a unique story. Every solution is tailored to match it.
Collaboration & Accountability
We partner with universities and stakeholders for shared, responsible success.
Positioning Framework
Mission
Empower students and professionals to achieve their dreams of studying and settling abroad — through expert guidance, tailored support, and innovative solutions.
Brand Tagline
"Winning is not a hope, but a habit."
Vision
The most trusted and forward-thinking global education consultancy — connecting ambitious students with top institutions and limitless opportunities.
02 — Logo System

Bilingual Usage Rule
When English and Bangla appear together, use Plus Jakarta Sans / DM Sans for English, Hind Siliguri for Bangla headlines, and Noto Sans Bengali for Bangla body copy. Never mix scripts in the same text run — keep them in separate elements. Always match weight levels across scripts.
05 — Voice & Tone

How We Sound

Confident
Not arrogant
We speak with authority because our 15+ years and 5,000+ students earn it. Never boastful. Always backed.
Warm
Not casual
Students trust us with their futures. We're human, invested, and genuinely rooting for each person's journey.
Empowering
Not prescriptive
We give students the tools to write their own story. We guide; we don't dictate. Their success is the point.
✓ Write Like This
✓ "We help you navigate the process — not just hand you paperwork."
✓ "Your success is what drives us forward."
✓ "5,000+ students have trusted us with their futures."
✓ "Winning is a habit we build — together."
✓ "From your first question to your first semester — we are there."
✕ Not Like This
✕ "We are the #1 consultancy in Bangladesh!" — unsubstantiated
✕ "Hey! We've totally got you covered bro." — too casual
✕ "Pursuant to your application, we hereby advise..." — too stiff
✕ "Don't worry, it's really simple!" — dismissive
✕ "100% visa guarantee!" — misleading, harmful to credibility
06 — Imagery & Visual Language

Visual Direction

Photography is how we show students their future — not just describe it. Every image must feel real, aspirational, and rooted in lived experience.

✓ Use
  • ✓ Real students in real moments — celebrating, studying, arriving abroad
  • ✓ Genuine emotion: joy, focus, pride, quiet determination
  • ✓ Diverse student profiles — gender, background, age
  • ✓ Authentic campus and city environments at natural daylight
  • ✓ Bangladesh-to-world transitions — familiar to aspirational
  • ✓ Warm, high-contrast, optimistic colour grading
✕ Avoid
  • ✕ Generic stock photos with posed, unnatural expressions
  • ✕ Imagery lacking South Asian representation
  • ✕ Dark, desaturated, or heavily filtered photography
  • ✕ Illustrated characters used as substitutes for real people
  • ✕ Cluttered backgrounds that compete with the subject
  • ✕ Imagery that centres Western students as the default
07 — Brand Ecosystem

Edumig & ELP

Edumig and ELP are sibling brands under the same parent. They serve different stages of the same student journey.

ELP by Edumig

Students begin here. ELP builds the English foundation — IELTS, PTE, spoken English — that makes the global dream achievable. 15,000+ enrolled students.

Global Education Consultancy — The Destination
ELP graduates transition here. Edumig handles university selection, applications, visa processing, and full end-to-end support to study or settle abroad.

Shared Elements
  • Brand Green #109951 as primary accent
  • Black as structural anchor
  • Plus Jakarta Sans + DM Sans + Hind Siliguri
  • Aspirational, empowering tone of voice
Distinct Elements
  • ELP uses Lime Green #A2FE2C — Edumig does not
  • ELP logo is organic/blob-shaped; Edumig is a sharp wordmark
  • ELP tone: encouraging, skill-focused
  • Edumig tone: authoritative, outcome-focused
  • Logos must never be combined into one lockup
